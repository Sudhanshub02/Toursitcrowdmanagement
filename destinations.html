Top Tourist Spots

Rishikesh

The yoga capital of the world, nestled in the Himalayas and known for its spiritual vibes and river rafting.

Neelkanth Temple

A sacred Hindu temple located in the dense forests near Rishikesh, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Ganga Aarti - Haridwar

One of the most spiritual experiences, the evening Ganga Aarti on the ghats is a must-see.

Mussoorie

A beautiful hill station often called the 'Queen of the Hills', perfect for scenic views and pleasant weather.
